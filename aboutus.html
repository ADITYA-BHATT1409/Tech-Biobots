At NASA, our mission extends beyond scientific discovery. We are committed to leveraging science, innovation, and technology to address some of the worldâ€™s most pressing challenges on Earth. As part of the global effort to achieve the United Nations 2030 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), NASA is using its expertise to contribute to goals that focus on environmental sustainability, climate action, clean energy, and innovation.

NASA is dedicated to applying cutting-edge technologies and research to address these global challenges. By aligning our efforts with the Sustainable Development Goals, we are committed to working with global partners, scientists, and educators to inspire and empower future generations to tackle the challenges of today and create a sustainable and equitable future for all.

We are dedicated to integrating education about the SDGs into existing science units, such as weather, geology, and soil health. By doing so, we aim to create a holistic learning environment where students can connect scientific concepts to real-world issues. Our goal is to inspire critical thinking, encourage collaborative problem-solving, and foster a sense of responsibility for our planet and society.
